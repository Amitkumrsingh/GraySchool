NABARD Coaching

Gray school provides the best NABARD coaching for all the aspirants striving to change the details in the agricultural world. NABARD is the highest development financial institution in India, headquartered in Mumbai and branches spread across the country. NABARD recruits at the Grade A (Assistant Manager) level and Grade B (Manager) level for the Rural Development Banking Service (RDBS). The bank has been entrusted to deal with all the matters with concern to the policy, planning, and operations in the field of credit for agriculture and other economic activities in rural areas in India". It acts as a refinancing agency for the institutes providing investment and production credit for the promotion of several development programs for rural development. NABARD also prepares rural credit plans annually for all the districts in India. It promotes research in the field of agriculture, rural banking and rural development.



We have 3 different phases as follows:


Phase 1:

It is an online exam with objective type questions with seven different sections namely Quantitative Aptitude (20 marks), Reasoning ( 20 marks), English language (40 marks), Computer Knowledge (20 marks), Economics and social issues (40 marks), General awareness (20 marks) and Agriculture and Rural Development (40 marks). The total marks for this phase are 200 marks. There is sectional and overall cut off in this phase.

Phase 2:

In phase 2, we have two papers, i.e, paper 1 and paper 2 in this phase for Grade A and three papers for Grade B. Paper 1 is on English writing skills, paper 2 is about Economic and social issues (ESI) and paper 3 is about the Developmental economy. Statistics and finance and Management (FM). Each paper carries 100 marks with multiple choice questions and in the online mode.

Phase 3:

In phase 3, based on the results from phase 2, candidates are shortlisted for the interview. The interview is conducted for 25 marks for Grade A and 40 marks for Grade A. For the final merit list, the marks obtained in phase 2 and interview are added.
