About TS LAWCET

TS LAWCET 2019 abbreviates into Telangana state Law Common Entrance Test. LAWCET exam is a state level entrance exam which is held every year on behalf of Telangana State Council of Higher Education by Osmania University, Hyderabad. This test is conducted for admissions in LLB which is a 3 years and 5 years course. The exam will be conducted in computer-based mode. In the state of Telangana, the paper for May 26 has been scheduled across 12 cities by Osmania University.

LAWCET Eligibility

1. The candidate should be a resident of India and should satisfy all the local/ non-local status of Telangana.

2. There is no age limit for LLB/BL course (3 years and 5 years)

Qualifications

1. For 3 year LLB Course: The candidate must hold a graduation degree( 10+2+3) or equivalent degree from a recognized university of Telangana or any other with at least 45% aggregate marks

2. For 5 year LLB Course: The candidate must have passed 12th or any equivalent class exam from the recognized board of Telangana or any other board with atheist 45% aggregate.

TS LAWCET Exam pattern

The exam pattern consists of objective type questions to evaluate a candidate's aptitude and ability to pursue law as a career.

All the intermediate graduates can register online for Integated 5- year program whereas degree holders can apply online for 3 year program.

TS LAWCET Syllabus

This test is partitioned into three parts. The total number of questions sum up into 120. The duration of time us 90 minutes and Maximum marks raise upto 120.

1. Part A: Consists of 30 questions, each carrying 30 marks.
2. Part B: Consists of 30 questions, each carrying 30 marks.
3. Part C: Consists of 30 questions, each carrying 60 marks.

TS LAWCET Syllabus: Current affairs

This part of the LAWCET exam consists of 30 questions with each carrying one mark. The questions are mostly from the events that happened within the country and across the globe in the recent times. It covers the current affair topics like

Polity

Economics

Awards and achievements

Scientific, discoveries and Inventions, etc.


Note: Newspaper and news channels can act as the best reference for current affairs.

TS LAWCET Syllabus: Aptitude for study of law

Part C of the paper consists of 60 questions with one mark each and the questions are based on legal Aptitude. The questions are based on basic laws and constitution of the country and will mostly be of the elementary level.
