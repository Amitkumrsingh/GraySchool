Digital Marketing

Our methods and faculty

Our methods serve unique knowledge about different digital marketing topics that are taking a huge course in the internet world. We house trained professionals to teach and quench the thirst that is growing rapidly for digital marketing professionals. We understand that just knowing the strategies and concepts of digital marketing is not enough, which is why we schedule separate digital marketing classes for understanding the uses of tools we teach.

Our faculty is not only experienced in the field of teaching digital marketing but also, are digital marketers themselves. Gray school aims at enhancing your internet marketing skill-sets and to walk you through a digital marketing career path. .


What is digital marketing?

Just like traditional marketing, digital marketing aims at the promotion of services and products online. Almost everyone you sit and wake up with has a smart phone and access to internet, also has a social media account, social media advertising is an efficient way of digital marketing. Digital marketing is otherwise known as Online marketing, web marketing or Internet marketing.



Benefits of Digital marketing

Can keep track of customer's response

Go viral with the right content

More opportunities

Reach a large number of people

Trainings on Digital Marketing


Search Engine Optimisation(SEO) training in Hyderabad

In SEO training, As the name suggests, it involves techniques that help the search engines like Google, Yahoo, bing rank your website depending on a few parameters. Our SEO classes may include details on those parameters to have the relevant traffic for the websites.

Search Engine Marketing (SEM) training in Hyderabad

In SEM training, we teach you how to promote websites online for visibility when content is searched on the search engine to display the search engine page results(SERPs). SEM is usually done through paid advertising. SEM includes search engine optimization (SEO) to rewrite or adjust website content to achieve a higher ranking in search engine page results to improve per per click listings.

Social Media Marketing (SMM) training in Hyderabad

Half the world is connected through social media accounts like Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, and Pinterest. Social media marketing talks about marketing on these platforms using social content, conversations and commentaries. In social media marketing classes, you'll learn all about SMM, video marketing and the techniques to market your brand, website, etc on them.

Social Media Optimization (SMO) training in Hyderabad

In SMO training, SMO is done on social media accounts like Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, video sharing websites and pinterest to generate traffic and to increase publicity to the brands, products and services. You'll learn more about SMO and tools used in our SMO classes.
