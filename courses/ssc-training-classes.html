Posts under SSC Combined Graduate Level Examination (SSC CGL)

Staff Selection Commission conducts Combined Graduate Level Examination (SSC CGL) to recruit Assistant/SI/Inspector/Divisional Accountant/Auditor/Statistical Officer/Junior Accountant/Tax Assistant/UDCC/ Compiler for Central Civil Posts. The minimum educational qualification required for these posts is a graduation from a recognized university. The recruitment for these posts is done in departments of Central Secretariat Service / Ministry of Railways / Intelligence Bureau /Central Vigilance Commission / CBI and a few other Ministries / Ministry of Defense / Departments/ Ministry of External Affairs/ Organisations of the Central Govt.

Post for SSC CGLE

The posts have been placed in two groups, Group -B and Group-C. For Group-B posts, Interview and personality test is conducted. For Group-C posts, Interview and personality are not conducted depending on their grade pay and papers in examination/Interview.

    Normalization in SSC CHSL simply means there is no differential marking scheme based on the accuracy used during the marking. However, the normalized mark is based on the average marks, toppers marks and standard deviation.
