About RRB:

The Railway Recruitment Board conducts RRB exam every year to provide employment in Indian Railways across the country. Government announces vacancies for railway jobs, interest candidates can apply for it. The recruitment is done for various posts, both technical and non-technical categories. RRB exam syllabus

This exam is conducted online every year for many students who aspire for a career in Railways. It has the questions from following topics:

Technical Ability

General Intelligence and Reasoning Ability

Arithmetic

General awareness

Technical ability

RRB Exam pattern (Objective type)

The written exam consists of 150 questions to be answered in 2 hours. 2 sections in RRB JE exam and has negative marking for 1/3 marks.

General ability - 60 questions each

Technical Ability/General Science – 90 questions each

Railway Group D exam pattern

This pattern has 4 different sections. The exam has 100 objective questions to be answered in 90 minutes.

Mathematics -25 questions

General Awareness and Current Affairs- 20 questions

General science- 25 questions

General Intelligence and reasoning-30 questions





The five exams that RRB conducts are RRB ALP, RRB JE, RRB SSE, RRC GROUP D, and RRB NTPC. Learn about them in detail below.

  • RRB ALP:
    RRB Assistant Loco Pilot, this is the exam conducted for the train driver post. For the person who has finished the diploma course in electrical/ mechanical or electronic engineering can apply for this post.
    Age: The candidate has to be between 18 to 30 years.
  • RRB JE/SSE :
    a) For JE: JE exam is conducted for the appointment of Junior engineer in the various railway department The Junior engineer has to work under the senior engineering.
    Education eligibility:
    - Diploma in electrical/ mechanical/electronics/civil/IT
    - B.Sc in civil engineering
    Age: The candidate has to be between 18 to 25 years.


    b) For SSE: SSE exam is conducted for the appointment of Senior section engineer in various railway departments.
    Education Eligibility:
    - Candidate should have completed 4 years of Bachelor's degree in Mechanical/Civil/M.Sc/Electrical/Electronics/ Control and instrumentation engineering
    Age: The candidate's age has to be between 20 to 34 years.
  • RRC Group D :
    This exam is conducted for all the posts such as helper, porter, and Trackman. The candidate's age has to be between 18 to 33 years. The candidate should have passed 10th/ITI or any equivalent from a recognized board of study to apply for this post.
  • RRB NTPC :
    The exam is conducted for the non-technical categories. A single exam is conducted for 9 different positions. The 9 different positions are

    1. Traffic Apprentice
    2. Commercial Apprentice
    3. Enquiry-cum- reservation clerk
    4. Goods guard
    5. Senior Clerk cum Typist
    6. Junior Account Assistant cum typist
    7. Assistant station master
    8. Traffic assistant
    9. Senior Time keeper
