Elitmus Coaching

Gray school provides coaching for all the candidates looking for entry-level jobs through the eLitmus assessment test. We exercise the best teaching techniques and personal skills development programs to crack the pH test conducted by eLitmus and the interviews you attend thereafter.




About eLitmus

pH test or Hiring Potential test is an assessment test conducted by eLitmus Evaluation Private Limited which is an Indian company that helps companies in hiring employees for entry-level jobs. pH test is used as a criterion for hiring employees by the companies. The pH test consists of three parts such as Quantitative Aptitude, Reasoning section, and the verbal section. The results are declared in the formats of percentile score and total score. The hiring companies call the pH test-takers for interviews on the basis of their pH scores and the companies criteria.
