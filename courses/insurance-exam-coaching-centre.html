Insurance Exam Coaching

Gray school is the most feasible training institute for insurance exam. We help students crack all the Insurance exams with proper training in all the sections during the examination. The training for Insurance exams includes NIACL, NICL, GIC and OCIL. Before appearing for a competitive exam, one must have enough knowledge about the eligibility criteria, syllabus, examination pattern, the recent changes made in the exam pattern.




The selection process may differ according to the exams you choose to write and depending on the posts. The common selection process is as follows:

Preliminary Examination

Mains Examination

Language Proficiency Test

Final selection

Candidates who qualify in the Online main examination will be shortlisted and invited for Regional Language Test.

Eligibility Criteria

Minimum Age: 21 years old
Maximum Age: 30 years

Qualification

The minimum qualification needed for the candidate appearing for this exam should have a graduation degree from a recognized university or any equivalent qualification recognized by central government. The candidates must have passed in English as one of the subjects at SSC/HSC/Intermediate/ Graduation level. Candidates should have the certificate as a proof of passing the examination.
